About Construction Chemical

Construction chemicals are specialized chemicals that are used in the construction industry to improve the quality, durability, and performance of construction materials, structures, and systems. These chemicals are used to enhance the strength, flexibility, workability, and adhesion of various building materials such as concrete, mortar, grouts, adhesives, waterproofing compounds, sealants, and coatings.

Some common types of construction chemicals include:

Concrete admixtures

Used to improve the workability, durability, and strength of concrete.

Waterproofing chemicals

Used to prevent the penetration of water into building structures.

Flooring chemicals

Used to enhance the properties of floor surfaces such as durability, abrasion resistance, and chemical resistance.

Adhesives and sealants

Used to bond and seal various building materials such as tiles, wood, metal, and plastic.

Protective coatings

Used to protect building surfaces from environmental factors such as corrosion, UV radiation, and weathering.
